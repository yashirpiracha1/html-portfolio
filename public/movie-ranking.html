The Best Movies According to Yashir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

300

This is my favourite motivational movie. I love the strong character, iron mindset and a high sense of responsibility of King Leonidas.

Interstellar

Really cool movie about space. What I love the most is how relativity of time space is demonstrated in the movie.

Wolf of Wall Street

Super exciting movie. I like to think I can be as good a motivational speaker, salesman and wealthy as Jordan Belfort at his peak.
